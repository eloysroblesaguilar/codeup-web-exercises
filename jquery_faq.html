MARIO FAQS

Who created Mario?
Shigeru Miyamoto
When was Mario created?
Mario debuted as Jumpman in Donkey Kong in 1981
What was Mario's original occupation?
Mario was originally a carpenter, not a plumber. He has also appeared as a Doctor.
Where does Super Mario Bros fall in terms of Nintendo sales?
The original Super Mario Bros is actually second in sales to Wii Sports.
How many games has Mario appeared in?
Mario has appeared in over 200 games.
Who composed the soundtrack to Super Mario Bros?
Koji Kondo composed the soundtrack. The main theme topped billboard ringtone charts for 125 weeks.
Has Mario ever appeared as an antagonist?
Mario stars as the antagonist in the 1982 sequel to Donkey Kong, Donkey Kong Jr.
What was the first sports game Mario was in?
Mario made his first appearance in a sports game in the 1991 NES game, NES Open Tournament Golf.
What inspired Mario's design?
His looks are dictated by the graphical limitations of the 1980s. His mustache and overalls serve as ways to help the player see otherwise indistinguishable features.
Who voices Mario?
Mario has been voiced by Charles Martinet since 1995. He will be voiced by Chris Pratt in the 2022 Untitled Mario film.
